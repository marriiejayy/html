Problem Statement
Why smallholder farmers and buyers struggle in current market flows
AgroUSSD — bridging the gap
  • Limited internet access
    Many rural farmers only have basic phones and lack reliable internet, preventing use of smartphone apps or online marketplaces.
  • Middlemen reduce farmer profit
    Farmers often sell to intermediaries who capture the largest share of the final price, leaving farmers with a small margin.
  • Buyers struggle to source directly
    Restaurants and retailers face difficulty locating and contacting producers, leading to higher costs and lower-quality produce.
